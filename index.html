About Me

Lily Wolfson is a graduating senior at Northwestern University, where she has honed her skills in multimedia journalism. With a passion for storytelling that spans across written word, photography, and video production, she brings a unique perspective to every story she tells.

Her work combines traditional journalistic rigor with innovative digital storytelling techniques, creating compelling narratives that resonate with diverse audiences. From breaking news coverage to in-depth feature stories, Lily approaches each assignment with curiosity, empathy, and a commitment to truth.

As she prepares to enter investment banking, she will continue to hone her photojournalism skills in her free time.
